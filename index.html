Rooted in Tradition, Inspired by Innovation

Our Indian Brahmin heritage is the foundation of our distinctive culinary tradition. Since 1989, we've been dedicated to sharing our passion for authentic South Indian flavors with the world.

  • Our recipes are rooted in traditional South Indian cuisine, passed down through generations of Brahmin cooks.
  • We blend time-honored techniques with modern flavors and presentations, creating a unique culinary experience.
  • Our commitment to using only the finest ingredients and adhering to stringent quality standards ensures a consistently exceptional taste.

For generations, we've perfected the art of cooking in South India, where flavor, texture, and nutrition come together in perfect harmony. Our recipes, passed down through generations, have been revived with innovative presentations and flavors, garnering acclaim from thousands of customers. We're deeply grateful for your support and look forward to continuing to serve you with love and care. Join us in celebrating our legacy and savoring the taste of tradition.

Made using - Tamarind, Jaggery, Salt, Red chilly powder, Pepper, Coriander, Cumin, Fenugreek, Mustard, Asafoetida, Curry Leaf, Groundnut Oil

A sacred offering, born from the divine will of the gods. This revered paste is a testament to the culinary wisdom of our ancestors, who revered the art of cooking as a sacred ritual. As we partake of its divine flavor, we are transported to the sacred temples of Melukote, where our forebears once performed pujas and offerings. The aroma wafts like the sacred smoke of a yajna, carrying our prayers and gratitude to the heavens. With every bite, we honor the traditions of our ancestors, who believed that the act of cooking was a form of worship. May this Puliyogre Gojuu guide us on our own journey towards spiritual growth and culinary enlightenment, as we delight in its flavor and bask in the blessings of the divine. It is a prasada, a divine gift, that nourishes our body and soul.
